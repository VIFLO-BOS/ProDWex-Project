🤩 What Drives Us

We are a purpose driven platform focused on Professional Development, Skills Training, and Work Experience.

At ProDWEx, we’re more than just a training provider, we are a forward thinking, impact focused agency empowering students and young professionals across Nigeria. Our work is driven by insight, innovation, and the passion to prepare the next generation for the real world.

Our Goal:
ProDWEx - Professional Development & Work Experience is dedicated to equipping tertiary institution students across Nigeria and Africa with essential skills, certifications, and practical experiences to enhance their employability and professional growth.
Watch our story
Innovation-driven

At ProDWEx, innovation isn’t optional, it’s foundational. Our programs and platforms are built with forward thinking tools and teaching methods that prepare learners for the future of work.

Transparent approach

Transparency is more than a principle,it’s how we operate. From course selection to certification, we ensure every process is clear, fair, and student-centered. No hidden terms. No confusing systems. Just honest, accessible learning.

Accessible technology

We believe in inclusivity. That’s why our training programs and digital platforms are designed to be accessible to students from all walks of life—across institutions, regions, and abilities.

Collaborative teamwork

We know that lasting impact is achieved through partnership. At ProDWEx, our success is powered by the synergy of educators, industry mentors, training partners, and our dedicated learners working together toward a common goal.

Know About ProDWex

Got questions? We have answers

ProDWex (Professional Development and Work Experience) is a platform that trains, coaches, and mentors students from tertiary institutions across Nigeria and Africa. We provide reflective supervision and technical assistance to help members gain workplace mastery and accrue experience aligned with their academic studies and passions.
ProDWex offers a range of professional development courses, including:
  • Workplace Ethics & Culture
  • Project Management (PMP)
  • Human Resource Management (HRM)
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • Health, Safety & Environment (HSE I, II & III)
These courses are designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills and certifications.
To register, visit our registration page. Fill in your details and submit the form to become a member of ProDWex.
ProDWex collaborates with over 154 institutions across Nigeria and Africa. Notable member institutions include:
  • Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU)
  • University of Abuja (UOFA)
  • Delta State University (DELSU)
  • Covenant University (CU)
  • Ekiti State University (EKSU)
  • Institute of Management and Technology (IMT)
  • Osun State College of Health Technology (OSCOHT)
For a full list, visit our institution page.
For inquiries, you can reach us through our contact page. Alternatively, you can email us directly at prodwexhub@gmail.com.

ProDWex empowers students and professionals with practical skills and industry-ready training, shaping the future of workforce excellence across Africa.
