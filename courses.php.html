Management Courses

ProDWex management courses are designed to provide you with specific industry knowledge to be enterprising and innovative which are key skills for all types of graduate employment. You can choose from a wide range of management courses listed below:


Work Place Ethics & Culture

Learn about the internal environment of organisations and their objectives to increase your awareness and understanding of ethical issues in business, and to provide you with useful conceptual tools to guide analysis and decisions.

Project Management

Learn the application of knowledge, skills, tools and techniques to meet the requirements of a project at the specified time and on budget by understanding how to initiate, plan, execute, monitor & control and close projects

Human Resourses Mgt.

Learn the process of hiring and developing employees so that they become more valuable to the organization. It includes conducting job analyses, planning personnel needs, recruiting the right people for the job
