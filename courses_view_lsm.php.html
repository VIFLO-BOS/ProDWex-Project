COURSE DESCRIPTION

Welcome to the Leadership and Strategic Management training programme for students in leadership roles in tertiary institutions! As a leader, you are responsible for guiding your team towards success, but how can you ensure that you are doing it effectively? The answer lies in developing strong leadership and strategic management skills.

This course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of leadership and strategic management. You will learn about the different types of leadership styles, the benefits of leadership and strategic management, and the skills needed to be an effective leader. You will also learn how to create an effective leadership team, develop a strategic management plan, and tips for leading and strategically managing a team.

Our programme goes beyond just theory and provides you with practical exercises and quizzes to reinforce your learning. Upon completion of the course, you will also be required to take an exam and participate in an internship, allowing you to apply your newly acquired skills in a real-world setting.

As a leader, investing in your personal and professional growth is essential to the success of your team and organization. By registering for this course, you are taking a step towards becoming a more effective leader and strategist. Don't miss out on this opportunity to enhance your skills and take your leadership to the next level. Register today

Register today to explore how you can become a strategic leader in your organization today! There is a lot to cover so let's get started.
