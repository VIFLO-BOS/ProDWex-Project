About ProDWex

ProDWex (Professional development and Work Experience) provides a platform where members are trained, coached, mentored with reflective supervision, provided technical assistance to gain mastery of the work place and accrue the necessary experience in line with their course of study in school and or their passions. This is achieved through strategic partnership with Private, Government and federal Government establisments.


About ProDWex - PDTS Scholarship

Professional Development for Tertiary Institution Students - PDTS scholarship scheme which focuses on equipping tertiary institution students to become professionals in the fields of Human Resource Management, HRM and Customer Relationship Management, CRM and Project Management, PMP as youths in the country crave to have professional relevance in various industries, while some have passion to become owners of businesses and to develop trades and vocational skills. .


About Consultancy Service Centre, OAU

UNIFECS is the Consultancy Service Centre of Obafemi Awolowo University statutorily charged with the responsibility of making this expertise available to the Government, Industry and private individuals. We are duty bound to guard and protect the interest of our clients at all times for the simple reason that we have a solemn duty to uphold and protect the image and integrity of our owner - Obafemi Awolowo University. We always bring to bear on our work the most modern tool of science, technology and the humanities that will produce results capable of withstanding the rigours of scrutiny and verification anywhere in the world. At UNIFECS we provide our clients entire consultancy needs under one roof.
